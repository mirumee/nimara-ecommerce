# Content

## Outcome

Ecommerce operators can connect a supported invoicing or tax provider and obtain predictable
order documents and regional configuration without maintaining store-specific glue code.

## Discovery boundary

The initiative must first define which responsibilities belong to Saleor, Nimara, and an
external provider. Country-specific legal interpretation and a universal tax-calculation
engine are not assumed to belong in the storefront.

## Evidence to collect

- Regions and document workflows requested by active adopters.
- Existing Saleor capabilities and gaps.
- Minimum provider contract that avoids coupling domain types to one vendor.
