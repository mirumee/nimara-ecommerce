# Content

Capabilities are organized by durable user or system outcome rather than by route, work item,
or package. Each page records current behavior, limitations, and planned evolution.

- [Commerce Storefront](./Commerce%20Storefront.md) - localized customer shopping surfaces.
- [Catalog And Discovery](./Catalog%20And%20Discovery.md) - products, collections, search, PDP/PLP.
- [Product Reviews](./Product%20Reviews.md) - wired placeholder versus the planned verified-review system.
- [Cart And Checkout](./Cart%20And%20Checkout.md) - cart lifecycle, delivery, addresses, order creation.
- [Payments](./Payments.md) - standard Stripe and marketplace multi-vendor payments.
- [Customer Accounts](./Customer%20Accounts.md) - identity, profile, addresses, orders, returns.
- [Content Search And Localization](./Content%20Search%20And%20Localization.md) - replaceable CMS/search and locale routing.
- [Marketplace Operations](./Marketplace%20Operations.md) - vendor onboarding and catalog/order operations.
- [Ledger And Payouts](./Ledger%20And%20Payouts.md) - settlement ledger and Connect transfer batches.
- [Agentic Commerce](./Agentic%20Commerce.md) - ACP and UCP protocol surfaces.
- [Tracking Consent And Observability](./Tracking%20Consent%20And%20Observability.md) - consent-aware analytics and telemetry.
